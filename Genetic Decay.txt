Genetic Decay - Civic - Cannot be removed after game start.

"This race has suffered irreperable genome damage to the point that they cannot reproduce organically. Instead, they weave future generations in Biological Looms from banks of preserved genetic information."

- Pops do not grow naturally over time. Instead, gain access to a new building - Genetic Loom - that allows the creation of new pops.
- Leader Lifespan reduced by 10 years.
- Unlocks a Special Research Project to reverse the decay.
